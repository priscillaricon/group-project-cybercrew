Welcome to Community Water And Sanitation Intiatives.

Our mission is to ensure every household in our community has access to clean,safe water and proper sanitation facilities

Problem identified

  1. Water pollution problems in the community.
  2. Poor sanitation leading to diseases.

Many families in our community struggle with unsafe drinking water and inadequate sanitation facilities,leading to frequent outbreaks of water borne diseases

Objectives of the intiatives.

  1. To provide clean and safe water to every household.
  2. To improve sanitation and hygiene practices
  3. To educate communities about water conservation.
  4. To promote eco friendly waste management.

Activities

  1. Installing boreholes or water tanks
  2. Organizing hygiene education programmes
  3. Building latrines

Expected outcomes

  1. Reduction in waterborne diseases
  2. Sustainable water uses
  3. Improved health and wellbeing
